BRS - Bicycle Rental Services

BRS stands for Bicycle Rental Services. It is an Indian bicycle sharing platform, headquartered in Mangalore. The company was founded in 2023 by Rohit Kumar along with his 4 classmates. It currently operates in 3+ college campuses.

Using BRS, Customers can rent a bicycle very easily. BRS has a beautiful User Interface with makes it user friendly to use. Our Aim is to burn less fuel and cycle more.

Happy Cycling! 😃
